Welcome to my life!

My name is Eric! I'm a Software Engineer who studied Applied Mathematics & Computer Science. I love learning about interesting topics in developmental biology, genetics, and genomics. The joy I have for genetics and computer science inspired me to pursue technical backgrounds in both computational approaches and molecular biology. Naturally, I became a professional librarian who often generated high throughput sequencing libraries in the pursuit to capture connections between genetic mechanisms, regulatory elements and/or gene expression.

When I am not thinking about recombinant DNA and/or full stack cloud deployments, I enjoy power lifting, competitive badminton and blog about fashion on Pinterest. I am a terrible golfer who is consistentlly improving to one day be good enough to play a 18-hole round at Torrey Pines. I cheer for the Golden State Warriors and the San Francisco 49ers and absolutely love watching my favorite NBA and NFL teams competing head to head every week.

Living my best life in the Bay Area, San Francisco; but you might also find this city boy wondering the streets of Manhattan. Whether I'm experiencing setbacks or making an epic comeback, I prioritize being happy because life's too precious to spend even a single second unhappy. I try to be grateful for all the blessings in life and I dream about having the confidence to redefine the boundaries of success and hard work.

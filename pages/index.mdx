# Welcome to my life
</center>

My name is Eric! I'm a Software Engineer who studied Applied Mathematics & Computer Science and knows a little bit of biology. I love learning about interesting topics in [developmental biology](https://kingsley.stanford.edu/), genetics, and [genomics](https://www.vertgenlab.org/). The joy I have for genetics and computer science inspired me to pursue technical backgrounds in both computational and molecular genetic approaches in answering fundamental molecular genetic questions. I was a professional librarian who generated unconventional sequencing libraries in the pursuit to understand the connections between [genetic mechanisms](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2021.12.14.472698v1.full.pdf), [regulatory elements](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5793789/pdf/256.pdf) and [gene expression](https://www.science.org/doi/abs/10.1126/science.1232542).

When I am not thinking about recombinant DNA and/or deployments of full stack cloud applications, I enjoy [power lifting](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1pdQFtmabllpWwsR2FSZ--5bYzGLjCyTy/edit#gid=1040558878), competitive badminton and fashion blogging on [Pinterest](https://www.pinterest.com/edotau/redefinedfashiongame/). I am a terrible golfer who is consistentlly improving to one day be good enough to play a 18-hole round at [Torrey Pines](http://www.torreypinesgolfcourse.com/). I cheer for the Golden State Warriors and the San Francisco 49ers and absolutely love watching my favorite NBA and NFL teams competing head to head every week.

Currently living my best life somewhere, everywhere! I prioritize being happy because life's too precious to spend even a single second unhappy. I hope being who I am will start redefining the boundaries of happiness, success and hard work.
